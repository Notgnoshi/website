Rules

Here's what we decided on together. Since we're all friends, infractions will be handled on a case-by-case basis, and suggestions will be listened to.

  • Griefing in any manner is unacceptable. Since mob griefing is enabled, creeper craters, etc., should be repaired as soon as possible, and spawning Withers should be done in a controlled environment.
  • If you'd like to "claim" land, clearly post boundary markers and signs. If you're trying to find a place to build, follow this simple rule: "Don't be a dick."
    • “Don't be a dick.”
  • There will be no warps to or from anywhere.
  • The world will start off fairly small at a 3000x3000 block square centered at spawn. The world will be expanded, within reason, at player request and admin approval.
  • Reasonable farms are fine. Unreasonable farming includes, but is not limited to
    • Extended AFKing
    • Farms that cause lag.
  • Since we're all friends, let's try to treat each other with respect, even when we don't want to.
